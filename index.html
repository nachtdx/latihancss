ame

The Japanese word "ame" means both "rain" and "candy". These two words have completely different kanji characters but are written in the same way in
Japanese phonograms (hiragana and katakana). Although their pronunciation are a little different, it might be difficult for foreigners to recognize the difference.

sora

Sora means “sky” in Japanese.
Its chinese character (kanji) is written as 空, while its kana (japanese alphabet) is written as そら.
Usually when conjucted with other words, such as when you want to say “blue sky (青空／あおぞら)”, the word becomes pronounced as zora (ぞら).
